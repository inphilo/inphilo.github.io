1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the app, including your name, email address, and usage data such as app activity.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your data to provide and improve our services, send notifications, and ensure security. We also use anonymized data for analytics.

3. What Legal Bases Do We Rely On?

We rely on your consent, legitimate interest, and legal obligations to process your data under applicable laws like GDPR and others.

4. When and With Whom Do We Share?

We may share data with service providers and partners who help operate our app. We never sell your data.

5. Third-Party Websites

Our app may link to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience. See our Cookie Notice.

7. Social Logins

If you choose to log in via social platforms, we receive basic profile info as per your permission settings.

8. International Transfers

We may transfer data across borders in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this notice.

10. Keeping Your Information Safe

We use organizational and technical safeguards to protect your data, though no system is 100% secure.

11. Information from Minors

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us immediately.

12. Your Privacy Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at inphilo.contact@gmail.com.
